The Duffer Brothers

Stranger Things was created, written, and directed by the Duffer Brothers (Matt and Ross Duffer). They also serve as executive producers for the show alongside Shawn Levy and Dan Cohen.

"They developed the series as a mix of investigative drama alongside supernatural elements portrayed with horror, science fiction, and childlike sensibilities."

Setting the series in the 1980s, they created a massive homage to the pop culture of that decade. The show is heavily inspired by the classic cinematic and literary works of Steven Spielberg, John Carpenter, and Stephen King, capturing the magic of 80s sci-fi movies where ordinary kids find themselves in extraordinary situations.
