Genre & Themes

Stranger Things is a unique blend of several distinct genres that create its signature 80s nostalgic feel:

  1. Science Fiction: Deals with parallel dimensions (The Upside Down), top-secret government conspiracies, and mind-control experiments.
  2. Supernatural Horror: Features terrifying monsters like the Demogorgon and the Mind Flayer, drawing heavy inspiration from 80s horror icons like Stephen King.
  3. Investigative Mystery: Every season features a core mystery that forces the characters to play detective to uncover the truth.
  4. Coming-of-Age: At its core, it is a relatable story about a group of nerdy kids growing up, dealing with shifting friendships, first loves, and high school drama.
